Encrypting a message or file:

  1. Go to the TimeSeed tab and click "Generate" to create a new 50-character seed.
    (Or paste an existing one.)
  2. (Optional) Enter a Pepper Password for added security. Keep it private.
  3. Click "Submit Seed" to generate:
    - A daily password
    - A 6-month password
  4. Click "Use in LockIt" next to the password you want to use.
  5. Go to the LockIt tab.
  6. Type your message or upload a file (max 10MB) to encrypt it.
  7. Send the encrypted result to your recipient.

Decrypting a message or file:

  1. Go to the TimeSeed tab and enter the shared TimeSeed.
  2. (Optional) Enter the Pepper Password if it was agreed upon.
  3. Click "Submit Seed" to generate the passwords.
  4. Click "Use in LockIt" next to the correct password.
  5. Go to the LockIt tab.
  6. Paste or upload the encrypted content.
  7. Read or download the decrypted result.
