Why TimeSeed

Your keys and your words never leave your hands.

TimeSeed does all of its work inside your own browser. There is nothing to sign up for, nothing stored on a server, and no one in the middle who could ever read what you write.

🛡

No middlemen

No central server ever handles your message — you create and encrypt it offline, on your own device. No accounts, nothing to sign up for, and no company sitting between you and the people you write to.

💻

Works offline

Everything happens inside your browser. Disconnect from the internet entirely and TimeSeed still generates keys and encrypts your text and files.

⚛

Post-quantum ready

TimeSeed v3 adds ML-KEM, the official post-quantum standard, so your messages stay sealed even against future quantum computers.

LATEST • v3

TimeSeed v3

Create a random 50-character TimeSeed — that is your secret. From it, the date you choose and any pepper words you add, TimeSeed derives the actual encryption key. That derived key is never stored: the same inputs re-create it every time. Encrypt and decrypt text and files, all in your browser, online or off.

Under the hood: ML-KEM post-quantum encryption layered on proven Argon2 key derivation and AES-GCM.
