Welcome to Sovereign Encryption

A two-step tour.

This tool is free. If it helps you, please support development at timeseed.io/donate →

1

Create your identity

In Step 1 on the page, click Generate new identity. A keypair is created in your browser - the private key stays on your device.

  • Download the .se.key (private) file. It is your private key. Never share it.
  • Copy your public key and share it with anyone who wants to send to you.
  • Match the orange fingerprint to confirm "same identity as yesterday".
2

Send and read

To send: paste the recipient's public key in Step 2. Encrypt a message or a file. Share the blob or the .locked file via any messenger.

To read: paste the blob - or upload the .locked file - in Step 3. Click Decrypt.
